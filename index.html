The Gaiuss Trilogy

by NSXplus

A connected series of RPG Maker adventures. Each title exists in the same world, telling different stories with unique characters, while building toward a larger narrative.

The purpose for these games is to learn the RPG Maker engine and learning and understanding Game Design while making cool projects.

Cartuga: Definitive Edition

Released: April 2026 • Engine: RPG Maker MV
Originally released in 2017 as part of a course program's final, the first entry of The Gaiuss Trilogy, you play as a villager who always wanted to be an adventurer. Cartuga DE adds additional side content as well ass other small lore drops. DE also includes minor fixes and balance changes.
